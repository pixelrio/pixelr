About

I'm Gary Bhanot, founder of Pixelr.io Labs Inc, a seasoned professional in the dynamic intersection of web development, digital marketing, analytics, and data science. With a passion for leveraging technology for positive change, my journey has been defined by a decade of dedicated service to over 70+ non-profit organizations.

As a web developer, I've mastered the art of crafting digital landscapes that not only captivate audiences but also drive meaningful engagement. I am more than a strategist; I am a storyteller. Through targeted campaigns, compelling content, and strategic online presence, I've helped non-profits amplify their voices and extend their reach to make a lasting impact in the digital marketing space.

I specialize in unraveling the mysteries of data to empower non-profits with the knowledge they need to navigate their journey effectively. Data science is where I find the magic in information. From predictive modeling to machine learning, I harness the power of data to unlock new possibilities for non-profits, helping them make data-driven decisions that elevate their impact.
